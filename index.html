WHAT WE DO

Here at Code404 we specialise in mobile application development, primarily for the android market. We will be researching, designing and developing a highly customisable events and calendar sharing application, with the aim of bringing collaboration amongst work colleagues and friends even closer. Our application will provide users with the ability to connect and share calendars events and meetings instantly with simplistic integration with Google and Exchange/Outlook calendars.

Our aim of this development is to further facilitate and improve on social interaction, planning and organisation by internet based social communication with calendar based event/action creation and tracking.
