What's Up

Brought to you from Code404, What's Up. The new and innovative way for you to organise your every day life. Designed with you in mind, What's Up allows you to plan everything important in your day in the form of events. Not only that but you have the power to connect with your friends and families or even your colleagues everywhere.

Simplistic Organisation

Simplify your calendar workflow. No need to remember every single meeting, football game, parents evening any more. View all your events in an instant, whether it be in the monthly plan view or simply the scheduled view.

Share Anything & Everything

Need to share an event with someone? Easy. What about an entire calendar? Of course. You share a calendar with someone and they will stay up to date with everything that happens.

Event Based Communication

Quickly and easily message the members of your events using the in-built chat system. A convenient way to notify all those friends, family and colleagues relating to a particular event or occasion.

Use Your Existing Calendars

Import all your existing calendars and events straight from Google, Outlook and iCloud calendars. Continually sync'd to your existing services, you'll never miss anything again.
